Build something awesome with Open Data!

The Socrata Open Data API allows you to programatically access a wealth of open data resources from governments, non-profits, and NGOs around the world.

SODA is based on standard, RESTful paradigms, and SDKs are available for many popular languages, so it's very easy to get started. Click the link below and try a live example right now.

http://soda.demo.socrata.com/resource/earthquakes.json?$where=magnitude > 5.5
